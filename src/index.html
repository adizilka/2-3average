Adi & Michal's 2/3 average

This is an implementation of the game "2/3 average". 3 players send a bet of a number between 1 and 100, and ether as the bet. The winners are the players with a bet closest to 2/3 the average of all the bets, and they get all the ether that was sent. In case of error or one player not sending the right values, you will get your money back.
